# COVID-19 Vaccination Information for Patients

The Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine is now approved for patients 5 years and
older. **We strongly urge all of our patients 5 years and older to get the
COVID-19 vaccine.**

The Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine is a 2 dose vaccine given 21 days apart.
Immunity from the shot occurs 2 weeks after the 2nd dose.

We have vaccines available for our 5-11 year old patients. Please call to schedule an appointment. If our appointment time is not convenient for you, you can find appointment sites at [https://vaxfinder.mass.gov](https://vaxfinder.mass.gov)

## Current Information (Updated 2021-11-29)

1. [Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine information sheet (VIS)](https://www.fda.gov/media/153717/download)
1. CONSENT FORM — any patient younger than 18 years is required to show written
consent from a parent or guardian. The parent does not have to be present at the
vaccine appointment but the patient must show the consent form. You can
[download a consent form from mass.gov](https://www.mass.gov/lists/ma-consent-forms-for-people-under-18-years-of-age).
2. SIDE EFFECTS — the side effects of the Pfizer-BioNTech vaccine for patients
ages 5 and older are similar to side effects experienced by adults: pain, fever, chills,
fatigue. These side effects are more frequent after the 2nd vaccine. 
3. HOW TO BOOK AN APPOINTMENT: 
    * If your child is between ages 5-11 call our office at 617-232-2811 for a COVID vaccine.
    * Look on [https://vaxfinder.mass.gov](https://vaxfinder.mass.gov) to find a vaccination site near you.
    * If your child has ever had medical care at Boston Children’s Hospital, you can
sign up for your child’s COVID vaccine at Boston Children’s Hospital. You can 
find more information here: bostonchildrens.org/covid19
